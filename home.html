About

MI Gardens is a venture that specializes in providing space and organising the different elements that make up for a great party. Although, MI Gardens offer a range of services for it's clients, it specializes in hosting traditional Indian ceremonies and rituals like a wedding. The place offers an outdoor and indoor space where the indoor space. For Bigger events, the indoor space, which houses rich interiors, serves as the eatery. Smaller events can be organised in the indoor space too, if the client wishes so.

Services

Adding to the industry standards, some of the interesting services MI Gardens provide an event of any size are

  • Valet Parking
  • DJ
  • Catering
  • Theme setting
  • Alcohol

There are endless possibilities to the services provided by MI Gardens according to the Client's budget. We'll be delighted to let you know what we can offer you, just give us a call.
